Security
Data & Security
Factual information about how your data is handled when you use our tools.

Encrypted in transit & at rest

All data is encrypted using 256-bit TLS encryption in transit and AES-256 at rest. Hosted on Google Cloud Platform infrastructure via Streamlit Cloud, owned by Snowflake.

Not stored or retained

Files you upload are processed in-memory during your session only. We do not store, retain, or have access to your financial data after your session ends.

Transparent application code

Our tools run on our own Python code — no black boxes. The application logic is entirely ours and is available for review upon request.

Private deployment available

For clients with stricter data requirements we offer private deployment on your own infrastructure. Your data never leaves your environment. Contact us to discuss options.

Our tools are hosted on Streamlit Community Cloud. Apps are accessible to anyone with the link — we recommend not uploading personally identifiable information. For full security details visit streamlit.io/security.
